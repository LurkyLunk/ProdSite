Create a Retro FPS Game in Unity

About the Book

Learn how to create your own retro first-person shooter (FPS) game using Unity. This book provides step-by-step instructions and hands-on projects to guide you through the process of designing and developing a classic-style FPS game with pixelated graphics and fast-paced gameplay.

Discover the techniques for creating retro-style levels, implementing player movement and shooting mechanics, designing enemy AI, adding power-ups and weapons, and much more. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced Unity developer, this book will help you bring your vision of a nostalgic FPS game to life.

Key Features

  • Learn Unity fundamentals and retro game design principles
  • Create pixel art assets and design retro-style levels
  • Implement player movement, shooting mechanics, and physics
  • Design enemy AI behaviors and create challenging encounters
  • Add power-ups, collectibles, and weapon upgrades
  • Enhance your game with retro sound effects and music
  • Optimize performance and publish your game for various platforms
